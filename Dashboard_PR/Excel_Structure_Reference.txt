DATA EXTRACTION NOTES
================================================================================

1. All 22 District 1 branches start at Row 5 (after 4-row header)
2. Rows 5-26 contain the 22 individual branches
3. Always-On data in Column 16 is only available in ม.ค.69 sheet
4. Data values are raw decimals (0-1 scale) - multiply by 100 for percentage
5. Water bill amounts are in Thai Baht (THB)
6. Payment totals represent customers who paid during the month
7. Each branch appears as a separate row with all monthly data
